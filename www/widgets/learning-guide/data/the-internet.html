<the-Internet>



The Internet is often described as a "cloud", an amorphous entity that is everywhere and nowhere at the same time. This sort of magical thinking does us a disservice as netizens. If we hope to make meaningful work with/about the Internet it's important to understand what the Internet actually is.



"Internet \in"ter*net, n. A deeply misunderstood technology upon which we increasingly depend " —Julian Oliver & Danja Vasiliev



That's not the text-book definition, but internet artists Julian Oliver and Danja Vasiliev make an important point. To better understand what the Internet is we should view it from different perspectives...
