a technical perspective

We're currently working on an interactive lesson which illustrates the journey a web-packet takes as it traverses the physical Internet around the world. Along the way we'll learn about various pieces of Internet infrastructure as well as explore how artists and activists have produced all sorts of creative works and interventions stemming from this fundamental understanding of what the Internet actually is.



In the meantime, you can explore Nick Briz's Internet Travelogue class notes.
